Subjects

Access restricted to registered students

Study materials are available for the following units:.

WEB Development

Web Development aims to train professionals capable of designing and implementing Web applications and services with multimedia content, independently or in a team, using new technologies and tools.

Artificial intelligence

The course analyzes the most known algorithms and their applications to problem solving. The main focus is on rationalization with knowledge through representations of Propositional and 1st order logics, use of structured methods for automated decision making, with probabilistic models and resulting inferences.

Project management

Understanding the role of Projects and Project Management in organizations and use of MS Project in the different components of a project, phases, activities, tasks, sub-tasks, milestones, restrictions and calendars.

Management Control

It is intended to reflect on the definition of effective instruments to manage the performance of organizations, ensuring the pursuit of strategic and operational objectives, avoiding dysfunctional attitudes and behaviors.

Systems and Networks Management

Acquire the methods and concepts that lead to an effective and efficient management of heterogeneous distributed systems and provide students with adequate knowledge for the elaboration of implementation plans and monitoring of a network of systems
